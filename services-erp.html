Process

From Discovery to Go-Live

Our proven four-phase delivery framework removes the risk from ERP projects — giving you clarity, control, and confidence at every stage.

1

Assessment

We audit your existing processes, data flows, and pain points — then recommend the ERP platform and modules that best fit your business goals and budget.

2

Configuration

Our engineers configure the system to your workflows, migrate historical data, integrate third-party tools, and run rigorous testing cycles before any user touches the platform.

3

Training

Role-based training sessions ensure every team member — from the CEO to the warehouse operator — knows exactly how to use the system on day one.

4

Go-Live & Support

We manage a smooth go-live with on-site hypercare support, then transition to our ongoing managed support plan to keep your system optimised as your business scales.

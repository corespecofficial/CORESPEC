Frequently Asked Questions

Is our company data secure with external AI? expand_more
Security is our priority. We utilize enterprise-grade API wrappers that ensure your data is never used to train public models. For high-security needs, we deploy private local LLM environments.
What is the average ROI timeframe? expand_more
Most automation projects see a positive ROI within 3 to 6 months by drastically reducing human hours spent on low-value repetitive tasks.
How do you handle staff resistance to AI? expand_more
Our training focuses on "Augmentation, not Replacement." We teach staff how AI can remove the "boring" parts of their jobs, making them more valuable high-level contributors.
Do we need a technical team for this? expand_more
No. We build user-friendly interfaces (Dashboards, Slack bots, or custom portals) that allow non-technical teams to interact with powerful AI logic effortlessly.

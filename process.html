FAQ

Common Questions

How long do your training programmes take? expand_more

Depending on the format: instructor-led programmes typically run 4–8 weeks, weekend bootcamps run 8–12 weeks, and online self-paced courses run 3–6 months. The right format depends on your goals and schedule — contact us to discuss.

How much do programmes cost? expand_more

Programme fees vary by service area and delivery format. Contact us at info@corespecng.com or call 08033395470 for current pricing and available payment plans.

Do I need prior experience to enrol? expand_more

Most programmes are designed for beginners and career-changers — no experience is required. Some advanced courses require a basic foundation in IT; specific requirements are listed on each individual service page.

What certifications will I earn? expand_more

Cloud: AWS Cloud Practitioner, Azure Fundamentals, Google Associate Cloud Engineer, CompTIA Cloud+. Cybersecurity: CEH, CISSP, CompTIA Security+. See each service page for the full list of certifications covered in that programme.

Do you offer corporate / group training? expand_more

Yes. We deliver on-site and online corporate training programmes that are fully customised for your team's size, schedule, and skill gaps. Contact us to discuss your organisation's specific needs and we'll put together a proposal.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Introduction

At Corespec Engineering, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email, and project details via our contact forms. We also collect technical data like IP addresses and browsing behavior using cookies to improve your experience.

3. How We Use Informtion

Your information is used to provide our services, communicate with you about your projects, and analyze website performance for optimization.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. Please contact us at hello@corespec.engineering for any privacy-related inquiries.

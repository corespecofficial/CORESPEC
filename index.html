FAQ

Common questions.

Are your certifications globally recognised? add

Yes. Our training programs are aligned to CompTIA, EC-Council, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud certification tracks. Graduates sit official exams at accredited testing centres and receive vendor-issued credentials accepted worldwide.

What training formats do you offer? add

We offer instructor-led classes (Mon–Fri), weekend bootcamps for working professionals, and online self-paced learning — so you can train around your schedule and location anywhere in Nigeria.

Do you offer corporate group training? add

Yes. We regularly deliver on-site and virtual training for corporate teams across Nigeria. Contact us at info@corespecng.com to discuss a custom program for your organisation.

Where is Corespec located? add

Our main office and training centre is in 4 Ikorodu Rd, Jibowu, Lagos 100001, Nigeria. We are open Monday to Friday, 8am–5pm. Online and weekend programs are available nationwide across Nigeria.

What is the AI Integration service? add

Beyond training, Corespec helps businesses implement AI into their operations — automating workflows, deploying intelligent document processing, building chatbots, and more. Contact us for a free AI Readiness Audit to see where AI can add the most value to your business.
